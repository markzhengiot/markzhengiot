Technology Behind Ultra‑Long Standby Trackers

Ultra‑long standby GPS trackers rely on high‑capacity batteries, energy‑efficient chipsets and low‑power wide‑area (LPWA) connectivity such as LTE‑M and NB‑IoT. By waking only at scheduled intervals to send small packets of data, these devices can operate for years without charging. Advanced power management, multi‑constellation GNSS, Wi‑Fi and Bluetooth ensure accurate positioning even in challenging environments. Sensors for temperature, humidity, shock and light allow a single tracker to monitor both location and conditions.

Applications Across Industries

  • Supply Chain & Logistics – Ensure continuous visibility of containers, trailers, pallets and returnable packaging as they travel across borders.
  • Cold Chain & Pharmaceuticals – Maintain temperature integrity and comply with regulations for vaccines, biologics and perishables.
  • Fleet & Asset Management – Track vehicles, heavy equipment and rental assets to optimize utilization and reduce theft.
  • Emergency & Safety – Provide location and status updates for lone workers, disaster relief equipment or remote sensors.
