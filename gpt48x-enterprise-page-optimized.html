Frequently Asked Questions

The GPT48-X achieves up to 5 years of battery life with one position report per day in Long Standby Mode. Actual battery life depends on reporting frequency, network conditions, temperature, and usage of Emergency Mode. In typical deployments with daily reporting and good network coverage, customers regularly achieve 3-5 years of operation.
The GPT48-X supports LTE-M and NB-IoT networks across 17 frequency bands, providing coverage in over 180 countries. Major supported regions include North America, Europe, Asia-Pacific, and Latin America. The device automatically selects the best available network. Check with your local carrier for specific LTE-M/NB-IoT availability.
The GPT48-X uses a non-rechargeable primary lithium battery that is not user-replaceable. This design ensures IP65 water resistance and eliminates maintenance requirements during the device's operational life. When the battery is depleted after several years, the entire unit should be replaced. Volume customers may inquire about refurbishment programs.
For enterprise customers, we offer flexible ordering options starting from pilot deployments of 10 units. Volume pricing tiers are available at 100, 500, 1000, and 5000+ units. Contact our sales team for custom pricing on large deployments and multi-year contracts.
The GPT48-X achieves 2-5 meter accuracy in open sky conditions using multi-constellation GNSS (GPS, GLONASS, BeiDou). In challenging environments like urban canyons or under light cover, accuracy may decrease to 10-15 meters. When GNSS is unavailable, the device falls back to LBS positioning with 100-200 meter accuracy in urban areas.
The GPT48-X offers multiple integration options: REST API for custom platforms, EELINK Protocol 2.0/2.1 for direct integration, compatibility with major fleet management platforms (Wialon, Navixy, Traccar), and webhook support for event notifications. Our professional services team can assist with custom integrations for enterprise deployments.
