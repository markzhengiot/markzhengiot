GPT50 Ultra-Long GPS Tracker

Revolutionary breakthrough in long-term asset tracking with unprecedented 10-year battery life. Built with 24,000 mAh lithium battery and power-optimized electronics for global deployment.

  • Global GNSS positioning (GPS, GLONASS, BeiDou, Galileo)
  • 4G LTE Cat-1 connectivity with 2G fallback
  • IPX7 waterproof and rugged design
  • Motion-triggered tamper alerts and light sensors
  • Emergency mode for real-time tracking

GPT29 Smart Monitoring

Comprehensive in-transit monitoring device with multi-sensor approach for real-time shipment conditions and environmental alerts. Perfect for cold chain integrity.

  • Temperature, humidity, shock, and light sensors
  • 4G LTE Cat-M1, NB-IoT connectivity
  • GPS, Wi-Fi, and BLE 5.0 positioning
  • One-year rechargeable battery life
  • Real-time condition data streaming
  • Predictive analytics for spoilage prevention
Applications

DB01 BLE Sensor Beacon

  • 54,000 historical records storage
  • iBeacon compatible with real-time alerts
  • IP65 protection rating
Perfect For

Cold storage, pharmaceutical compliance, food safety, laboratory monitoring
