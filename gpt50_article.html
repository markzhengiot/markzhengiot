Why GPT50 Is the Most Low‑Maintenance Tracker for Asset Managers

GPT50 is designed to minimize the operational burden of asset tracking: a 24 000 mAh battery delivering up to 10‑year standby, multi‑GNSS positioning and LTE Cat‑1 with 2G fallback keep devices connected in more places, while smart sensor alarms reduce human intervention. The tracker is IPX7 waterproof, operates from −20 °C to +60 °C and weighs roughly 350 g (±10 g).

Key Reasons GPT50 Requires Less Maintenance

Decade‑Long Power
24 000 mAh primary battery with deep sleep + duty‑cycle reporting enables up to 10‑year standby and greatly reduces charging logistics.
Reliable Positioning
GNSS (GPS/GLONASS/BeiDou/Galileo) plus cell‑based fallback for challenging environments.
Global Connectivity
LTE Cat‑1 with 2G fallback keeps reporting in regions where 2G is still the safety net.
Rugged & Weather‑Ready
IPX7 waterproof rating and shock‑resistant housing protect the device in outdoor operations.
Smart Alarms
Light, temperature, or motion‑based alerts help intervene only when required.
Service‑Friendly
Simple mounting, remote config and OTA options shorten field time.

Recommended Use Cases

  • High‑value returnable assets (containers, crates, tools, rental equipment)
  • Long‑haul logistics where battery swaps are impractical
  • Cold‑chain cargo requiring condition awareness
  • Outdoor storage and construction yards with harsh weather

Typical Specs (One‑Page Summary)

  • Battery: 24 000 mAh; up to 10‑year standby

For asset managers, fewer truck rolls, fewer battery swaps, and fewer touch points directly translate into lower total cost of ownership. GPT50 helps you keep visibility high while maintenance stays low.
